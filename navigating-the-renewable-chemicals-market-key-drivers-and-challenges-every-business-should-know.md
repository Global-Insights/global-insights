These bio-based chemicals help reduce reliance on fossil fuels and lower greenhouse gas emissions, addressing environmental concerns and supporting the transition to a circular economy.</p>
<p>The market is being driven by advancements in biotechnology and chemical manufacturing, which have improved the efficiency and cost-effectiveness of producing renewable chemicals. Moreover, policies such as India&rsquo;s&nbsp;<strong>BioE3 policy</strong>, which encourages the use of biological resources for bio-based chemical production, are fueling market growth. The growing global push to reduce emissions and the rising awareness of environmental sustainability are also key factors contributing to the market expansion.</p>
