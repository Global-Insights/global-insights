<p data-start="640" data-end="1215"><strong data-start="640" data-end="685">What is a Heat Pump and How Does It Work?</strong>&nbsp;A heat pump is an eco-friendly device designed to efficiently move heat from one place to another. It functions as both a heater and an air conditioner, making it a versatile option for year-round comfort. In&nbsp;<strong data-start="895" data-end="911">heating mode</strong>, it extracts heat from the outside air or ground and brings it inside. In&nbsp;<strong data-start="986" data-end="1002">cooling mode</strong>, the process reverses, expelling heat from the interior to the outdoors. This efficient energy transfer system reduces reliance on traditional heating and cooling methods, offering a more sustainable alternative.</p>
