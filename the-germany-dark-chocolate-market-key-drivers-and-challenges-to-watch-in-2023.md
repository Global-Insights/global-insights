Dark chocolate, made from cocoa butter, sweeteners, and cocoa solids, is renowned for its rich, bittersweet flavor. In its unsweetened form, dark chocolate contains no added sugar, offering a more intense and pure taste. Beyond its flavor, dark chocolate is a source of essential minerals like zinc, iron, and magnesium, and is known for its numerous health benefits, including reducing inflammation and lowering the risk of heart disease. Packed with antioxidants such as flavanols and polyphenols, dark chocolate helps neutralize free radicals and prevent oxidative stress, supporting overall wellness.</p>
