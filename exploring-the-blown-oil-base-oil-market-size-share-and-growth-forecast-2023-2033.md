<h3>What Are Blown Oil Base Oils?</h3>
<p>Blown oil base oils are created by heating natural oils like castor, soybean, and linseed oil with air-oxygen streams. This process improves the oils' viscosity, oxidation stability, and film strength, making them essential for creating high-quality lubricants. As the automotive industry and industrial sectors expand, the demand for these advanced lubricants grows, driving market growth.</p>
