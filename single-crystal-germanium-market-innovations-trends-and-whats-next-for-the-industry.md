<h2>What is Single Crystal Germanium?</h2>
<p>Single crystal germanium is a high-purity, uniform material prized for its unique physical and chemical properties. It is widely used in advanced technologies, particularly in&nbsp;<strong>electronics, photonics, and infrared (IR) systems</strong>&nbsp;operating in the&nbsp;<strong>2&ndash;12 &micro;m range</strong>. Its applications include lenses, windows, and other components due to its mechanical toughness, hardness, and environmental resilience.</p>
