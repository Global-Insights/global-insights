<p data-start="532" data-end="881">COB LEDs are lighting modules where multiple LED chips are mounted onto a single substrate, creating a compact and efficient unit. <p data-start="1075" data-end="1261"><strong data-start="1075" data-end="1105">Technological Advancements</strong>: Continued developments in COB LED technology, such as improved heat dissipation and longer lifespan, are enhancing their performance while reducing costs.</p>
