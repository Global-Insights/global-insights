<p><a href="https://www.sphericalinsights.com/reports/extreme-lateral-interbody-fusion-surgery-market" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>Extreme Lateral Interbody Fusion (XLIF)</strong></a>&nbsp;is a minimally invasive spinal surgery that accesses the spine from the side of the body to treat conditions like degenerative disc disease and spinal stenosis. The surgeon removes damaged discs and fuses the affected vertebrae, stabilizing the spine and reducing pain. This technique uses smaller incisions, leading to less postoperative pain, shorter hospital stays, and faster recovery compared to traditional spine surgery. XLIF is especially beneficial for elderly patients, offering a safer and more efficient treatment option for spinal conditions.</p>
