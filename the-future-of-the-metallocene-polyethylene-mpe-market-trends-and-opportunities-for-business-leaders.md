<h2><strong>What is Metallocene Polyethylene (mPE)?</strong></h2>
<p>Metallocene polyethylene (mPE) is a high-performance polymer produced using&nbsp;<strong>metallocene catalysts</strong>, which offer superior control over polymer structure. This results in enhanced properties such as&nbsp;<strong>toughness, clarity, flexibility, and puncture resistance</strong>. mPE is widely used in industries like&nbsp;<strong>packaging, healthcare, automotive, and consumer goods</strong>&nbsp;due to its exceptional performance and recyclability.</p>
