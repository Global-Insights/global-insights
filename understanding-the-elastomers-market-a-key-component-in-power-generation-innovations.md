The demand for elastomers in the automotive sector is particularly significant due to their durability, impact strength, and resistance to oil, grease, and ozone. Furthermore, recent developments in renewable elastomers are driving market growth, as these bio-based alternatives offer similar performance to conventional synthetic options while promoting biodegradability and reducing carbon footprints. The rise of electric vehicles and an increasing preference for bio-based products are also contributing to the market's expansion.</p>
