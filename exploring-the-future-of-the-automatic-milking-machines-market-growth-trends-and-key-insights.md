<p><strong>What Are Automatic Milking Machines?</strong></p>
<p>Automated milking systems, also known as&nbsp;<em>robotic milking</em>, leverage advanced technologies such as robotic arms, teat-cleaning systems, and sensor technology to milk dairy animals, especially cows, without human intervention. These systems rely on specialized software and computers to manage herd health, monitor productivity, and ensure precise milking operations. As dairy farms evolve into more complex businesses, automated milking systems have become essential to maintain sustainability, efficiency, and competitiveness in the industry.</p>
