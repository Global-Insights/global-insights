They are essential in industries such as manufacturing, energy production, and construction, where they are used in reactors, ovens, heaters, and high-temperature boilers. Additionally, the rise in construction activities in the UK, where refractories are used in building kilns, furnaces, and other infrastructure, is expected to drive market growth in the coming years.</p>
