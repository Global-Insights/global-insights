<h2><strong>What Are Floating Drilling Rigs?</strong></h2>
<p>Floating drilling rigs are specialized offshore platforms used to explore and extract oil and gas reserves in deepwater or harsh marine environments. These rigs are anchored to the seabed and are essential for accessing deeper offshore fields as onshore reserves diminish. They play a critical role in meeting global energy demands while adhering to safety and regulatory standards.&nbsp;</p>
