The market is witnessing rapid growth due to the increasing demand for advanced defense technologies, with APS playing a crucial role in protecting armored vehicles, aircraft, and naval vessels from missile and projectile threats in real-time. As geopolitical tensions rise and defense forces modernize, the need for these systems has escalated. The integration of cutting-edge radar, infrared sensors, and countermeasures is further enhancing APS capabilities. Key players in the market are pushing for innovation, causing a shift from traditional passive armor solutions to more dynamic, responsive protection systems. The market is expected to expand further with the rise of global defense budgets.</p>
